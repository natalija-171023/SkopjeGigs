Elektricni Orgazm

24.06.2020


20:00h


Stadium ARM, Skopje

The famous Serbian rock band Elektricni Orgazm back in Skopje with a new date - 24.06.2020 at ARM Stadium, OPEN AIR!

Originally starting as a combination of new wave, punk rock and post-punk, the band later slowly changed their style, becoming a mainstream rock act. They were one of the most notable acts of the former Yugoslav rock scene.

The OPEN AIR concert will be one day before the Skopje Gori festival, where the legendary Placebo will perform. The band will perform on the big stage and even more production, with a birthday repertoire that includes all the songs that marked the career of this superior band.
