Bond

25.09.2020


20:00h


Macedonian Philharmonic, Skopje

It is a great pleasure to announce the first big world concert for 2020 - The most popular and best-selling women's string quartet of all time, BOND, is coming to Skopje for the first time with a gala concert on March 20 at the Macedonian Philharmonic.

The BOND quartet is a superbrand with a million-strong army of fans from all around the world. Their music is a great act of defiance with styles. They are considered the best-selling string quartet of all time. They draw inspiration from classical, Latin, folk, jazz, rock, electro, Indian and Middle Eastern styles. Since its debut, the quartet, also known as "Spice Girls of Classical Music", has sold an astonishing 5 million albums worldwide, with 56 platinum and 15 gold copies on its releases.

Tickets are already on sale and you can get them exclusively at Avalon music & ticket store in GTC, Avalon sales network across the country and online at avalon.mk
