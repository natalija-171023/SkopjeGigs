About us

SkopjeGIGS is a website that shows you all of the upcoming music events in Skopje, Macedonia. Our site provides all of the details and informations about the events. Whether your tastes are classical, rock and roll or something else entirely, you're likely to find something great. Explore the music list for upcoming Skopje concerts and other live music events.

We are working directly with the official concert and production companies in Macedonia and the region, providing our customers with the latest news and information on all of the events.

WE STRIVE TO POST THE MOST CURRENT AND ACCURATE EVENT INFORMATION, BUT CHANGES AND CANCELLATIONS DO OCCUR, SO PLEASE CHECK THE OFFICIAL EVENT WEBSITE TO CONFIRM ALL DETAILS BEFORE ATTENDING.
